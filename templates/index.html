Step 1 — Language preferences Choose the spoken language for accurate transcription, and the output language for the note.
Optional: speed & accuracy tips

Leave Speaker language on Auto-detect for best multilingual results. If the transcript looks wrong (mixed scripts / random words), manually choose the language you spoke and retry.

If cloud STT is not configured, the app falls back to local Whisper. For Telugu/Indian languages, larger Whisper models in .env can improve accuracy; WHISPER_MODEL=base and WHISPER_FAST=1 favor speed.

Step 2a — Upload audio Upload a recording from your device (recommended for best quality).

Formats: WAV, MP3, M4A, WebM, OGG.

Step 2b — Record in browser Use your microphone for a quick intake (quiet room recommended).

Recording is not supported in this browser. Use upload instead.

Allow the microphone when asked → Stop when done → Generate note.
